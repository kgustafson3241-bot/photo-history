Key Milestones:

  • Camera Obscura – Projected images
  • 1826/27 – Earliest surviving photograph
  • 1839 – Daguerreotype announced
  • 1888 – Kodak roll-film camera
  • 1900 – Brownie camera for the public

Welcome to Origins of Photography. Our mission is to highlight the groundbreaking inventions that made modern photography possible.

Photography began with simple optical discoveries and evolved into one of the most powerful tools for communication and storytelling. Early pioneers experimented with light-sensitive chemicals and lenses to permanently capture moments in time.
